who

I'm someone who makes things. Sometimes those things are useful. Sometimes they're just interesting. Often both, hopefully.

I've been building at the intersection of creativity and craft — not chasing trends, just following curiosity until it becomes something real.

how I work

Slowly and deliberately. I like to understand things deeply before committing. I'm drawn to work that has a point of view — design that means something, writing that moves, code that's elegant.

I care a lot about detail. The kind people don't consciously notice but would miss if it were gone.
